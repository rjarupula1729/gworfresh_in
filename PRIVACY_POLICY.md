# GrowFresh — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 16 May 2026_

GrowFresh ("we", "our", "the app") is an Indian home-gardening and
ecommerce mobile application. We respect your privacy. This policy
explains what we collect, why, and your rights.

## 1. Data we collect

| Category | Examples | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Account identifiers | Mobile number, OTP | Sign-in, account recovery |
| Profile (optional) | Name, email, city, avatar, family members | Personalise the app |
| Health profile (optional) | Age, conditions, dietary goals | Recommend plants/products suited to your health |
| Location (optional) | Approximate location (city) | Show region-appropriate plants and delivery options |
| Purchase data | Orders, cart, payments | Fulfilment, order history, analytics |
| App diagnostics | Crash logs, performance metrics | Improve stability |

We do **not** collect: contacts, SMS, photos, calendar, microphone, or
precise GPS coordinates.

## 2. How we use it
- Provide the core service (catalogue, ordering, plant tracking).
- Suggest products based on what you've bought or your health profile.
- Analyse aggregate buying behaviour to plan our product line-up.

## 3. Sharing
We do **not** sell your personal data.
We share data only with:
- **Payment gateways** (e.g. Razorpay) – for processing transactions.
- **Logistics partners** – name, phone, address for delivery only.
- **Cloud hosts** – Render.com / AWS / GCP, who store our database
  under standard security agreements.

## 4. Storage & security
- Data is stored in encrypted PostgreSQL databases hosted in
  Singapore/India regions.
- All traffic to our API is TLS 1.2+ (HTTPS).
- Passwords aren't used – we authenticate via mobile OTP.

## 5. Retention
- Account data: kept while your account is active.
- Order data: kept for 7 years to meet Indian tax/legal requirements.
- Anonymised analytics: kept indefinitely.

## 6. Your rights
You can at any time:
- View / edit / delete your profile inside the app.
- Request full account deletion by emailing **support@growfresh.app**.
  We'll erase or anonymise within 30 days.

## 7. Children
The app is not directed at children under 13.

## 8. Changes
We will notify you in-app of any material changes to this policy.
